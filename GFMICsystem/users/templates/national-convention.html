Annual National Convention



ADVISORY

The Government Financial Management Innovators Circle (GFMIC), Inc. would like to inform everyone that the 12th Annual National Convention (ANC) scheduled on June 23-25, 2021 is rescheduled on July 21-23, 2021 due to inevitable circumstances.


ABOUT

The Government Financial Management Innovators Circle (GFMIC), Inc., an organization of government financial management professionals who utilize information and communications technology as a tool for productivity, transparency and accountability, will be conducting its 12th Annual National Convention on July 21-23, 2021 with the theme:

“Ensuring Business Continuity: Innovative Fiscal and Administrative Measures in Addressing the Pandemic”.

In this regard, we are cordially inviting the heads and personnel of the finance, budget, accounting, treasury, procurement, information and communications technology and allied offices of National Government Agencies, Government Corporations and Local Government Units, including the Barangays (Barangay Chairman, Kagawads, Treasurer, Secretary and Record Keeper) to attend this Convention.

The registration fee for the three-day convention is P1,500.00 per participant and may be deposited to any Philippine National Bank Branch under GFMIC, Inc. PNB Account No. 1102-7000-2003 or to any Land Bank Branch under GFMIC, Inc. LBP No. 1691-1707-28. Please visit the GFMIC website www.gfmic.ph on how to register to the said Convention and/or to be a member.

For inquiries and/or confirmation, please contact GFMIC at mobile phone numbers 0916-677-2956 or 0917-825-8675.

We look forward to seeing you and thank you very much for your continued support.


TOPICS FOR DISCUSSION

Day 1: July 21, 2021 (8:30am-12nn)

Resilience in times of Crisis: Pasig City Best Practice

Financing Government Response to COVID 19 Pandemic

Day 2: July 22, 2021 (8:30am-12nn)

Updates on the Revised Chart of Accounts for National Government Agencies

Feel Pag-Ibig Anytime: Benefit Updates

Day 3: July 23, 2021 (8:30am-12nn)

Compensability of COVID 19 under the Employees’ Compensation Commission

The BITBo Updates & Sharing the Municipality of Tanay Experience
