13th Annual National Convention

Greetings from GFMIC, Inc. (formerly Electronic Financials Users’ Circle (EUC), Inc.)!

The GFMIC, Inc. will be holding its 13th Annual National Convention via the virtual platform (Zoom) on August 10 to 12, 2022, with the theme: “Pursuing PFM Excellence: Onto the New Administration”. During this event, the GFMIC, in recognition of the significant accomplishments and contributions of users of the electronic New Government Accounting System (eNGAS) and eBudget System for the period July 1, 2021 to May 31, 2022, will be awarding agencies under the following categories:

     1. eNGAS and eBudget System Inclusiveness Award (formerly Gallantry Award);

     2. Grassroot Implementer Award; and

     3. eNGAS and eBudget System Collaborator/Contributor Award.

The mechanics, deadlines and eligibility criteria for the foregoing awards are attached for your reference (Annex A). In view of the above, GFMIC requests that your agency submit the self-Nomination Form (Annex B), together with the documents to support the nomination, to the Audit Team Leader for validation and endorsement to the Commission on Audit – Government Accountancy Sector for further verification and validation before submission to GFMIC for final evaluation.

Government Accountancy Sector for further verification and validation before submission to GFMIC for final evaluation.

The self-nomination process adopted by GFMIC provides a fair opportunity for all qualified agencies to be recipients of the Awards.

We look forward to your participation in this endeavor. Thank you!

This Week's News

Registration is now open for the ICGFM 2022 International Conference scheduled for September 19-23, 2022 in Washington, DC, USA!

The International Conference is designed to engage participants from all levels of government including ministers of finance, accountants, and auditors general, department heads, and other PFM professionals from across the globe as well as others in the PFM community. Keynote speakers, panels, research papers and other presentations will focus on common challenges and innovative approaches to address all aspects of the PFM cycle.


The 2022 International Conference has been re-imagined and reinvented!


Full program and speaker information coming soon!

  • 4 1/2 days of programming
  • 20 sessions designed to promote dialogue and discussions. Note: Sessions are in English.
  • 40+ speakers
  • Breakfasts and lunches included in the registration fee.
  • A new, intimate venue - ICGFM has exclusive use of the meeting space.

The conference will be held at The University Club of Washington, DC on historic 16th Street, just blocks from the White House, the U.S. State Department, The World Bank, and the IMF. The University Club offers fine dining, athletic facilities, including a swimming pool and squash courts as well as a spa.

ICGFM has reserved a limited block of rooms at The University Club at a special conference rate of $299++ single/double occupancy.
