Membership Conference



ABOUT

Greetings from GFMIC, Inc. (formerly Electronic Financials Users’ Circle (EUC), Inc.)!

GFMIC has launched a series of seven webinars this year 2020 via Zoom which started on October 6 and ended on November 17. This is the response of our organization to pursue its mission as premiere partner in capacity building of personnel of the financial management services and allied offices, amidst the health crisis brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic.

As a culminating activity, the 2020 Membership Conference shall be held from December 3-4, 2020 (morning) via Zoom. We are cordially inviting the heads and personnel of the finance, budget, accounting, treasury, procurement, information and communications technology, and allied offices of the national government agencies, government-owned or controlled corporations, and local government units, including the barangays.

Two interesting topics await the members/participants such as Updates from the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) on Budget and Treasury Management System and a topic on Digital Transformation to be delivered by a representative from Microsoft Philippines.

The highlight of the conference will be the awarding of citations to eNGAS/eBudget System user-agencies under different categories, as well as the oath-taking of GFMIC officers and new members.

ABOUT

The Government Financial Management Innovators Circle (GFMIC), Inc., an organization of government financial management professionals who utilize information and communications technology as a tool for productivity, transparency and accountability, is conducting its Annual Membership Conference, with the theme: “Financial Trends and Transformation in the New Normal” on November 18-19, 2021 via Zoom platform.

In this regard, we are cordially inviting the heads and personnel of the finance, budget, accounting, treasury, procurement, information and communications technology and allied offices of National Government Agencies, Government Corporations and Local Government Units, including the Barangays (Barangay Chairman, Kagawads, Treasurers, Secretaries and Record Keepers) to attend this Conference.

The registration fee for the two-day conference is P1,200.00 per participant and may be deposited under Government Financial Management Innovators Circle (GFMIC), Inc. to any Philippine National Bank Branch under PNB Current Account No. 1102-7000-2003 or to any Land Bank Branch under LBP Current Account No. 1691-1707-28. Please visit the GFMIC website at www.gfmic.ph on how to register to the said Conference and/or to be a member.

We look forward to your participation in our Membership Conference and thank you very much for your continued support.
